Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the City of Light. It is famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, romantic atmosphere, and world-class cuisine. From iconic landmarks to charming cafés, Paris offers an unforgettable travel experience.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark in Paris.
  2. The Louvre Museum – home to the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. The Champs-Élysées – a world-famous shopping avenue.
  5. Montmartre – known for its artistic history and beautiful views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – a buttery and flaky pastry.
  • Macarons – colorful and sweet meringue cookies.
  • Escargot – a traditional French delicacy.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Cheese – rich and flavorful varieties.
