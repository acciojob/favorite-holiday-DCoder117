Paris, France


Paris is known as the City of Light and is famous for its romantic atmosphere, culture, and historic landmarks.

  1. The Eiffel Tower is the most iconic landmark.
  2. The Louvre Museum houses the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral features stunning Gothic architecture.
  4. The Champs-Élysées is a famous shopping avenue.
  5. Montmartre offers beautiful city views.
  • Croissants are buttery pastries.
  • Macarons are colorful sweet treats.
  • Escargot is a traditional French delicacy.
  • Crêpes come with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Cheese has rich flavors.
